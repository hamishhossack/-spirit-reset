Escape the urban hustle
and dance free

 

What to expect

A beautiful dance space, great music and
the freedom to dance however you want.
It’s a sober event. Everyone is welcome.

 

When to come

Session starts at 8:00pm.
Latest you can arrive is about 8:30pm.
We finish at 10:00pm.

 

What to wear

Loose fit, gym or yoga wear is good.
Be ready to dance and sweat a little.
Wear what makes you feel happy and comfy.

 

How to pay

Cash on the door is good and entry is £10.
No card payments yet, but you can pay
online in advance (coming soon...)
